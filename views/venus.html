Facts about Venus

  • Venus is the second planet from the sun.
  • One day on Venus is equivalent to 243 Earth Days.
  • One solar revolution takes 225 Earth days.
  • Its radius is 3,760 miles.
  • Surface temperature can reach 880° F (which is hotter than Mercury!).
  • Venus is about 0.7 AU from the sun (for reference 1 AU is the Earth distance from the sun).
  • Venus rotates backwards compared to Earth.
