Facts about Mars

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the sun.
  • One solar revolution takes approximately 660 Earth days (sols).
  • One rotation takes 24.6 hours.
  • Mars has two moons Phobos and Deimos.
  • Mars has a radius of 2,106 miles
  • Mars appears mostly red due to oxidation of iron in the rocks.
  • Temeratures on Mars range from -225°F to 70°F.
