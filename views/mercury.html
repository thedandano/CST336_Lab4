Facts about Mercury

  • It it is the first planet from the sun.
  • Mercury is 2.6 times smaller than Earth.
  • One day on Mercury lasts 176 Earth days.
  • One revolution around the sun takes 88 Earth days.
  • Its radius is 1,516 miles.
  • Mercury does not have any moons.
  • Temperatures on the surface range between 800°F to -290°F.
  • It is about 0.4 AU away from the sun (for reference 1 AU is the Earth distance from the sun).
