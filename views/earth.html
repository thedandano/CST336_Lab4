Facts about Earth

  • Earth is the third planet from the sun.
  • It is 94,494,689 miles from the sun (or 1 AU).
  • 1 day on Earth is about 23.9 hours.
  • One revolution takes about 365.25 days
  • Earth has one Moon.
  • Earth radius is 3,959 miles.
  • Earth's surface is made up of 70% water.
  • It is the only planet (that we know of) that is host to many different forms of life.
